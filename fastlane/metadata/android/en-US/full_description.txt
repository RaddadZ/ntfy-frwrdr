A privacy-first Android notification and call forwarder that pushes your phone's notifications and incoming calls alerts to a self-hosted ntfy server. 

Nothing leaves your device except what you explicitly configure to be forwarded, and only to your own server. No hardcoded app list, no cloud dependency, and no third-party analytics.

Features:
* Dynamic app selection: Searchable picker of all installed apps
* Rich notifications: Per-app emoji tags, icons, markdown, and OTP copy buttons
* OTP verification codes: Forward as-is, redact codes, or skip entirely
* Incoming call alert forwarding: Optional contact name resolution
* Smart log retention: Auto-delete on success with configurable retention
* Security hardened: HTTPS enforcement and AES-256-GCM encryption for all secrets
